**Conflict named** — Point 2 (global signal-hub autoload) contradicts **godot-composition rule 5** (no autoload/service-locator for cross-entity wiring), **godot-main-scene** (autoload is a data bag, no signals/logic) and **MEMORY: Health is a signal entity, not centralized**. Do NOT adopt the event-bus autoload here. Our equivalent is parent-wires-at-spawn + `@export` injection + duck-typed seams.
